Mohamad Quteifan, Data Scientist

My journey to Data Science was a wild one and one that I am thankful for every day. I will begin by sharing a powerful, and life-altering realization: life is far too short to pursue a career that denies personal happiness and fulfillment. This epiphany found its roots in 2019 when graduating from The Ohio State University. At the time, I was determined to entrench myself within the medical profession and found myself on the cusp of entering medical school. Upon reflection, I realized that chasing after a career that I lack interest in would not lead to any memorable creation or innovation. I then examined my own life, relentlessly searching for that thing that brought me the most excitement, energy, and euphoria. Therein, I discovered my passion in the form of data science and programming. This may seem odd to the casual onlooker, but I sincerely love this career, for nothing is more reflective of this data-driven world in which we live.
